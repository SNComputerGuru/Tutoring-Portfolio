Who We Are

GradeBoost is a dedicated tutoring company committed to helping high school learners excel academically.

Our primary focus is on matriculants,
as we understand the pivotal role that the final year of high school plays in shaping a student’s future.

We aim to bridge the gap for learners who have the potential to achieve great results
but require additional support to unlock their full capabilities.

Our mission is to empower learners with the knowledge and skills
needed to qualify for their desired courses and secure admission to top universities.

At GradeBoost, we firmly believe that academic excellence is
achievable with the right guidance, mentorship, and commitment.

Meet Our Expert Tutors

Our team comprises top-performing Wits University students who excelled in their matric year and are passionate about education.
With firsthand experience of what it takes to succeed academically,
our tutors are equipped to provide learners with practical strategies and comprehensive subject
knowledge to achieve exceptional results.
